To Inspire 1 million lives

As a 30 year old thriving with bipolar, I realized that mental health awareness needs to be addressed from perspective of one fighting it. Being an educator, writer and enterpreneur and more than that a human being, I believe and wish anybody dealing with any issues on spectrum of mental health illnesses must recieve the right help and at the right time. Also, I believe that each one of us is equally intelligent and creaive just that its in different fields. My aim through my startup is to reach a million lives and more with respect to living better, living healthier and living stronger than ever before
